Flows

A flow is the automation attached to a stage transition — it pulls work from one stage, runs a graph of agent and shell steps, and lands the card on the next stage when it succeeds. Flows are versioned: every save bumps the version, and in-flight runs keep the version they started on.

Flows are off until you turn them on

This board ships with the Spec, Plan and Code defaults, but nothing runs automatically yet — every card waits for a human. Turn a flow on to start handing its stage to the AI. Cut over one flow at a time; you can always turn it back off.
