HOW IT WORKS

Every stage has an owner. The work moves between them.

You decide which stages people run and which the AI runs. Finished work lands in a Done column, ready for the next owner to pull it forward.

You stay in control

Human stages are yours. Queue work, review AI output, approve or send it back — nothing ships without a person.

The AI does the work

AI stages pull ready work automatically, show live progress, and hand finished cards forward — you watch it happen.

It asks before it guesses

When the AI hits a decision it can't make, the card blocks and waits. You answer inline; it picks up where it left off.

WHEN IT'S UNSURE

A question, not a wrong guess.

The AI would rather ask than assume. Blocked cards surface right on the board with a clear prompt — the whole team can see what's waiting on a human, and for how long.
